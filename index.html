A Coastal Story Reimagined

Coco & Spice was born from Goa's shoreline kitchens, where coconut, seafood, and layered spices create deeply comforting food with global appeal.

As we launch in China, we stay true to Goan roots while designing experiences for local tastes, dining habits, and modern urban life.

What Is Goan Cuisine?

Goan cuisine is a coastal Indian style known for bright flavors, slow-cooked spice pastes, and coconut in many forms, from milk to toasted flesh.

At Coco & Spice, each dish balances sea freshness with aromatic heat, making it easy for first-time guests to explore and enjoy.
